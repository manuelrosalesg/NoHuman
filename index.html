NoHuman

If you look at social media and porn adiction they have two things in common:

  • Watching humans
  • Infinite scroll

What im trying to build is the best way to remove those things from the computer, while still being able to use your phone for other things. Basically not blocking a whole app, but the things that make people consumers rather than creators.

Anyone can colaborate to make a better product, and start helping internet addicts.

Free and open source.
