NoHuman

If you look at the 10 things most people are addicted in their computers, at least 9 of those ten has one of these two things in common:

  • Watching humans
  • Infinite scroll

What im trying to build is the best way to remove those things from the computer, while still being able to use your phone for other things. Basicly not blocking a whole app, but the things that make the app addictive.

Anyone can colaborate to make a better product, and start helping internet addicts.

Free and open source.
